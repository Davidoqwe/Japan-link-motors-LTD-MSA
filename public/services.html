Our Import Process

How we bring your dream car from Japan to Kenya

Step 1: Vehicle Selection

Browse our inventory or let us source the exact make, model, year, and specifications you're looking for from auctions.

Step 2: Auction & Purchase

We bid and purchase your chosen vehicle from auctions. You receive the full auction sheet, inspection report, and grade evaluation.

Step 3: Shipping & Logistics

Your vehicle is shipped from Japan to Mombasa Port. We handle all shipping arrangements, insurance, and documentation.

Step 4: KRA Clearance

We manage all customs clearance, duty assessment, and tax payment with KRA. Your vehicle is fully cleared and compliant.

Step 5: Inspection & Delivery

Final mechanical inspection, detailing, and preparation. Your car is delivered to our showroom or directly to your doorstep.
