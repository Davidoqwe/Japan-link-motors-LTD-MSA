About Japan Link Motors LTD MSA

Mombasa's trusted car dealer

Our Story

Japan Link Motors LTD MSA was founded with a simple mission — to bring the best of automotive engineering to the Kenyan market with transparency, integrity, and exceptional service.

Based on Moi Avenue in the heart of Mombasa, we have grown from a small dealership into one of the Coast region's most trusted names for imported vehicles. Over the past decade, we have helped thousands of Kenyans find their perfect car, from fuel-efficient hatchbacks for city driving to rugged 4x4s for off-road adventures.

Our team combines deep knowledge of the auto auction market with a passion for customer service. Every vehicle we sell is hand-picked, thoroughly inspected, and fully KRA-cleared before it reaches you.

Our Mission

To provide every Kenyan with access to high-quality, reliable vehicles at fair prices, backed by professional service and unwavering integrity. We strive to make the car buying experience transparent, simple, and enjoyable.

Our Vision

To be Kenya's most trusted automotive brand — recognized for quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction in every vehicle we sell and every relationship we build.

Our Milestones

2014 — Founded

Japan Link Motors was established on Moi Avenue, Mombasa, with a vision to provide quality imported vehicles to the Kenyan market.

2016 — 500 Vehicles Sold

Celebrated the sale of our 500th vehicle. Expanded our showroom and workshop facilities to serve more customers.

2019 — 1,500+ Happy Customers

Reached the milestone of 1,500 vehicles sold. Expanded services to include maintenance, spare parts, and vehicle export.

2022 — Regional Expansion

Extended our reach to serve customers in Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda. Partnered with leading banks to offer financing options.

2024 — 2,000+ Vehicles Delivered

Surpassed 2,000 vehicles delivered. Launched our online platform to serve customers nationwide.
